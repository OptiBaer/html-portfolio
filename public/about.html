About Me

Christian Kuhlmann: From Culinary Creations to Digital Craft

Hey there! I'm Christian Kuhlmann, a self-taught web developer from Berlin, Germany. While my journey might not be your typical developer story, it's definitely fueled by a passion for creation and a love for learning.

For most of my career, I thrived in the world of fine dining, spending 9 years honing my skills as a chef. But after a while, I craved a new kind of creativity, one that blended problem-solving with the satisfaction of building something from scratch. That's when I discovered the exciting world of web development.

When I'm not glued to my code editor, you'll find me tending to my garden, enjoying life with my wife, or spoiling our adorable chihuahua. Yes, life's a delicious mix of digital adventures and real-world fun!

This blog is my outlet to share my experiences, learnings, and maybe even a few web development recipes along the way. So, whether you're a seasoned coder or a curious beginner, join me as I explore the ever-evolving world of web development!
